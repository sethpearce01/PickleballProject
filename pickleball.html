The Greatness of Pickleball

By Seth Pearce

Reasons Why Pickleball Is so Great

  1. Accessible
    • Can play on a tennis court, lots of new courts are being built!
  2. Not physically demanding
    • No physical contact with others, short distances to move!
  3. Anyone can play
    • Old people are the best at pickleball!

My Strengths

  1. My kitchen game
    • I can slam it good and control the front line!
  2. Painting the lines with my hits
    • I keep the opponents guessing if it is going to be in or out!
  3. Encouraging teammate in doubles
    • I cheer on and celebrate with my teammates after every play!

My Areas of Improvement

  1. My serves
    • I am consistent, but they are nothing special!
  2. Returning fast hits
    • I naturally flinch when a ball is flying right at me super fast!
  3. Lack of spin
    • I haven't figured out how to consistently put spin on the ball!
